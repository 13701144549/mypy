Native boolean operations
=========================

Operations on ``bool`` values that are listed here have fast,
optimized implementations.

Construction
------------

* ``True`` and ``False``
* ``bool(obj)``

Operators
---------

* ``b1 and b2``
* ``b1 or b2``
* ``not b``

Functions
---------

* ``any(expr for ... in ...)``
* ``all(expr for ... in ...)``
